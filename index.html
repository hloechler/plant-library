Snake Plant

(Dracaena Trifasciata)

The snake plant is a popular and hardy houseplant. It is in the family Asparagaceae and is native to tropical West Africa. The leaves of the plant purify air inside of your home by absorbing toxins. These plants attract prosperity, money, and good energy.
